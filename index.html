What PDEA Does

Video Player

Beautiful player with speed control, cinema mode, resume watching, and keyboard shortcuts.

Video Downloader

Download from YouTube at any quality — 360p to 8K. Playlists, batch, and audio-only supported.

Subscriptions

Follow YouTube channels, auto-detect new uploads, and auto-download with your preferred quality.

Ambience Mode

Mix background sounds — rain, wind, cafe, fire — each with its own volume. Perfect for focus.

Custom Themes

Dark, light, white — or build your own with custom colors, fonts, and layout settings.

Restricted Content

Password-protect folders. Hidden until unlocked. Session timer auto-locks after set time.
